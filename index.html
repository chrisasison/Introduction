I attended Washington State University (WSU) in 2012 but I ended up leaving after my junior year in 2015. I enlisted into the infantry and spent three years in the Army. At the time, I had no intention of going back to finish my college education. However, while I was on a deployment in Afghanistan, I had the opportunity to go back to school to finish my Bachelors and commission as an officer.

After my deployment in 2018, I went back to WSU. On May 15, 2020, I graduated with a B.S. in Psychology and commissioned as a Military Intelligence officer.

Since I left active duty, my life's had a couple of little changes...

Now I love to spend as much time as possible traveling and documenting our adventures.
